How it works

Log each team's remaining credit balance at three points per shift day (all CET): 9 AM, 12 noon, and 6 PM. Consumption is calculated automatically from the drop between readings — morning burn (9am→noon) and afternoon burn (noon→6pm). At $0.01 per credit, cost follows in USD and EUR. Pick a date and checkpoint above, enter the balances you read from lemlist, and save.
